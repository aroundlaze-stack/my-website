BUYING GUIDE

How to Choose the Right Computer

Keep these important factors in mind before making your purchase.

🧠

Choose the Right Processor

Intel Core i5/Ryzen 5 are excellent for most people, while i7, i9 or Ryzen 7/9 are ideal for gaming, programming and video editing.

💾

Get an SSD

An SSD dramatically improves boot times, application loading and overall responsiveness compared to traditional hard drives.

🧩

Don't Ignore RAM

16GB RAM is the sweet spot for most users. Gamers and creators should consider 32GB for smoother multitasking.

🔋

Battery Matters

If you travel often, choose laptops with at least 8–10 hours of real-world battery life.

🎮

Dedicated Graphics

For gaming, 3D work or video editing, choose a laptop with a dedicated NVIDIA or AMD GPU.

💰

Set a Budget

Spending more doesn't always mean better. Buy the machine that fits your workload, not just the highest specifications.
